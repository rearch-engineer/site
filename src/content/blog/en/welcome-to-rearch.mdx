# Introducing ReArch

ReArch is an AI background agent built to change how organizations ship code. Instead of limiting code contributions to engineers, ReArch lets everyone on your team — product managers, designers, and engineers alike — turn ideas into shipped code.

## How It Works

ReArch runs in sandboxed virtual machines with your full development environment. You describe what you want, and the agent writes the code, runs tests, and creates a pull request. You can close your laptop and check the results in the morning.

## Key Features

- **Background Execution** — Each session runs in its own sandboxed VM, working while you focus on other things
- **PR Creation & Review** — Automatically creates pull requests and responds to review feedback
- **Visual Verification** — Screenshots and live previews for frontend work
- **Multi-Repo Support** — Works across your entire codebase
- **Multiplayer Sessions** — Multiple team members collaborating in real-time
- **Unlimited Concurrency** — Run as many sessions as you want, simultaneously
- **Voice Support** — Talk to your agent naturally

## The Vision

The bottleneck in most organizations is not ideas — it is the ability to turn those ideas into shipped code. ReArch removes that bottleneck by giving every person in your organization the tools to contribute.
